FAQs

What is CIRT?

The Criminology Institute of Research Technology (CIRT) is a platform for academic research and collaboration in criminology and criminal justice.

How do I register for an account?

You can register by clicking the "Register" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form.

How can I access journals and research materials?

Once logged in, you can navigate to the "Journals" section to explore available research materials.

Who can I contact for support?

If you need assistance, please contact our support team at support@cirt.utampa.edu.
